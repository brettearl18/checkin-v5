# Client Tracking & Motivation Guide

## Daily Tracking

### What to Track Every Day:
1. **Check-in Completion**
   - Complete assigned check-ins when they arrive
   - Answer all questions honestly
   - Add comments/notes when relevant

2. **Mood & Energy Levels**
   - Note how you're feeling
   - Track energy throughout the day
   - Identify patterns and triggers

3. **Basic Habits**
   - Sleep quality and duration
   - Hydration levels
   - Meal timing and quality

## Weekly Tracking

### What to Track Weekly:
1. **Progress Photos**
   - Take photos from front, back, and side views
   - Same time of day, same lighting
   - Same clothing (or none) for consistency
   - Upload to track visual progress

2. **Body Measurements**
   - Weight (same day, same time, same scale)
   - Waist circumference
   - Hip circumference
   - Chest/chest measurements
   - Any other relevant measurements

3. **Weekly Reflection**
   - What went well this week?
   - What challenges did you face?
   - What would you like to improve?

4. **Check-in Review**
   - Review your check-in responses
   - Look for patterns or trends
   - Celebrate small wins

## Monthly Tracking

### What to Track Monthly:
1. **Comprehensive Progress Review**
   - Compare current photos to previous month
   - Review measurement changes
   - Analyze check-in score trends
   - Review goal progress

2. **Goal Assessment**
   - Are your goals still relevant?
   - Do they need adjustment?
   - What new goals should you set?

3. **Program Engagement**
   - Review completion rates
   - Identify areas for improvement
   - Plan for the next month

## Motivation Tips

### Stay Motivated:
1. **Celebrate Small Wins**
   - Every check-in completed is a win
   - Every photo uploaded shows commitment
   - Every measurement taken is progress

2. **Track Consistently**
   - Set reminders for weekly photos
   - Make it part of your routine
   - Use the same conditions each time

3. **Review Your Progress**
   - Look back at where you started
   - Compare photos side-by-side
   - See how far you've come

4. **Stay Connected**
   - Communicate with your coach
   - Share challenges and successes
   - Ask for support when needed

5. **Be Patient**
   - Progress takes time
   - Small changes add up
   - Consistency is key

## Onboarding Checklist

### Initial Setup (Can be done over time):
- [ ] Upload before photos (front, back, side)
- [ ] Record starting body weight
- [ ] Record starting measurements
- [ ] Set initial health goals
- [ ] Complete first check-in

### Remember:
- You can skip any step and come back later
- It's better to start tracking now than wait for perfect conditions
- Your coach can help you with any step
- Progress is progress, no matter how small
